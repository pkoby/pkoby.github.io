Welcome to LGBTQ+map!


Find places that are friendly to the LGBTQ+ community. Use the  button to find your location, and click  to load points of interest that are tagged with lgbtq=* in OpenStreetMap.

Places will show as icons:

🛐 Church, 🏢 Office, 🏪 Shop, 🍸 Bar, 🍺 Pub, 🍝 Restaurant, ☕ Cafe, 🎭 Theatre, 🏺 Museum, 🧖 Sauna, 📚 Library, 📜 Memorial, or 🏳️‍🌈 Other.

Little icons indicate the welcomeness of a place, from 🌈 primarily for LGBTQ+, to 👍 welcome, to ⛔ prohibited.

Tap the icons to get more information or to edit a place in OpenStreetMap.
