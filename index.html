Maps

ArtMap
A map displaying public art in the OpenStreetMap database.
Details include type, artist, material, date, and photo.

BenchMap
A map displaying benches in the OpenStreetMap database.
Details include bench material and color, presence of back and armrests, and inscriptions.

LGBTQ+map
A map displaying places friendly to the LGBTQ+ community in the OpenStreetMap database.
Details include POI type and welcomeness.

PicMap
A map displaying features with photographs or that could be improved with one in the OpenStreetMap database.

Smith Estate Map
A map showing change in plat ownership across centuries in one area in Warrenton, VA.
